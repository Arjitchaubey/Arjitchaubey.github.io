28nm HKMG NMOS Characterization & PDK Calibration

Project Overview

Developed a physics-based model of a 28nm HKMG NMOS transistor, implementing Density Gradient Quantization and Trap-Assisted Tunneling models.

  • Calibration: Achieved >95% agreement in subthreshold and saturation regions against reference PDK models by tuning PhuMob and saturation velocity parameters.
  • Analysis: Executed 1 MHz AC Small-Signal Analysis to extract Gate Capacitance ($C_{gg}$) curves across Accumulation, Depletion, and Inversion regions.

5-Stage Pipelined RISC-V Processor

Designed and implemented a 32-bit 5-stage pipelined RISC-V (RV32I) processor in Vivado using verilog.

  • Performance: Synthesized at 100 MHz with timing closure (WNS of 4.472 ns), indicating a theoretical max frequency of ~180 MHz.
  • Efficiency: Optimized for 142 mW power consumption and ~1.5k FF resource utilization.
